Megrim is a privacy-first, offline-first migraine diary. It automatically enriches each logged
migraine with weather, barometric-pressure, and astronomical context — computed and stored
entirely on your device — and surfaces personal descriptive analytics plus odds-ratio "suspected
factors" correlations.

Why Megrim:

* No accounts, no server, no telemetry. The only network traffic is to Open-Meteo.com to fetch
  weather for the approximate (~1 km rounded) location and date of entries you create.
* Your data stays yours: on-device storage only, with full export and import (JSON and CSV).
* Automatic enrichment: weather, 24h/48h pressure change, sunrise/sunset, daylight hours, moon
  phase — no manual entry.
* Real personal analytics: trends by day-of-week, time-of-day, season, month, pressure change,
  and moon phase, plus a "suspected factors" card using odds ratios over your own log.
* Free and open source (GPL-3.0-or-later).

Megrim is a personal diary and is NOT a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or
prevent any condition. "Suspected factors" are statistical associations in your own log —
association is not causation. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Weather data by Open-Meteo.com.
