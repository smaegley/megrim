v0.3.0 — improvements since v0.2.0:

- Event Detail: record an aura description and notable foods on any entry.
- Export now offers a direct "Save to device" option alongside sharing, so a backup can be saved straight to your phone's storage.
- Analytics: a new "By year" chart shows how your migraine frequency has changed over the years you've tracked.
- Fixed a bug where editing an entry while offline could erase its already-fetched weather.
- Fixed weather accuracy for entries about a week old, and a couple of date-counting edge cases around daylight-saving time changes.
- Fixed importing entries with accented characters or emoji in notes and locations.
- Hardened the CSV export against a known spreadsheet-formula security issue.
